Twelve Cups Cafe
by Santa Claus | 21 June 2018

Twelve Cups welcomes cafe hoppers with its new menus as well as a fresher, simple yet beautiful interior design. Twelve Cups is hungry to prove themselves that they do serve superb main courses too, ranging from breakfast, brunch, pasta, new desserts and crepe cakes. Guess what, we were impressed!


It has a really nice and simple interior. White walls, wooden tables and chairs, everything makes up a perfect spot for a food photograph. However, one thing that affected our dining experience was the cafe’s temperature, it was warmer than expected.

 AVOCADO EGG MAYO SANDWICH - RM21.90, healthy pick of the day, Avocado Egg Mayo Sandwich was what wowed me during this visit, I mean, it was perfect! Avocado sandwich is technically just sliced or smashed avocado on top of a piece of toast, so simple you think? But when you take one bite you will quickly realize that it is so much more than that. In addition, it topped with lots of herbs, great idea, and bacon slices was seared appropriately and on point. THE PINE WAFFLES – RM 17.50, there’s no better way to beat the hot weather than by tucking into a waffle & ice cream at brunch. Meet The Pine Waffles, Twelve Cups’ masterpiece. It filled with tiramisu, vanilla ice cream, shaved chocolate and espresso.

In short, A big thumb up for Twelve Cups’ Avocado Egg Mayo. More than that, the food and coffee here were great as well. We, Penang Foodie highly recommend you visit to here if you want to experience healthy yet delicious breakfast and brunch meals. Ping the staffs for recommendation based on your preferences!.
